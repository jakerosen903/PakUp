Pack Easy

What we do

Packing is our product!

→ We are a complete turn-key service. We will take care of your entire moving out process; this includes everything related to the packing, storage, and shipping processes, as well as all returns.

→ We will send in our specialized team, whom we call "The Muscle" at the time of our customers’ choosing. Sit back and relax while they operate the move-out process.

→ Making your move-out stress and labor-free is our goal.

Who are we

→ We founded PakUp as a student-run business created BY Duke students FOR Duke students.

→ We recognize the potential problems and stresses that COVID-19 introduces to our community when we are forced to move out at the semester’s during finals.

→ We believe that PakUp is a community-saving idea that will facilitate an effortless and stress-free move-out and storage process for everyone.

→ We want to strengthen our community; make friends with “The Muscle” while they take care of your move out.

→ We care about social impact: PakUp guarantees a donation of 10% of all profits to Feeding America: a charity that operates a network of 200 food banks and 60,000 food pantries across the US, and is currently accepting additional donations to its COVID-19 Response Fund here.

How it works

1.

Choose a bundle that fits your unique needs and schedule a move-out date

2.

Label what belongings are to be shipped and what belongings are to be stored

3.

We come and take care of any and all packing, moving, shipping and storage of your belongings

4.

You sit back and relax while we make the process of moving out of your dorm room as easy and stress-free as possible
